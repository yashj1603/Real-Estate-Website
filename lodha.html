Gracing the centre of Pune’s most sought-after location, and ensconced in 3 acres of gorgeous greens, Lodha Estilo brings an unprecedented lifestyle to Kharadi. A rare confluence of lush vegetation and thoughtful outdoor spaces, it makes for the perfect private haven.

An elite enclave. For an elite few

Limited 3 & 4-bed residences, ensconced in wooded greens, while 5-star hospitality services help run your day effortlessly. A trio of towers 27 storeys high, a grand clubhouse extending over 12,000 sqft, an al fresco sky deck, and other luxuries to indulge in – it is a privilege only a rare few will access.

Raising the bar of luxury – An Al fresco sky deck

The rarest of all luxuries, the terrace café beckons an elevated lifestyle.Set on a higher vantage point to absorb the calming greens, extending up to the horizon.There isn’t a better setting to gather with friends and family, or to enjoy a moment by yourself, relishing a serene sunset of pink

A landscape of luxury, obviously designed by maestros

Designed by Sitetectonix, esteemed landscapes artists from Singapore, it is embedded with a multitude of lawns and pavilions dedicated to work, or play, as you choose. While the infinity edge pool and its daybeds are seamlessly woven in for rejuvination and relaxation.

A tranquil symphony of lush greens and water bodies, crafted for an enriching experience, akin to a luxury resort

Shaded walkways and meandering orchard trails connects the wellness terrace and clubhouse, urging you to rekindle your relationship with Mother Nature. Stroll barefoot, in the company of birds and butterflies, or simply laze on the hammock, soaking in the cool breeze. You'll be left wondering if this isn't paradise, what is?

The ace of clubs

Overlooking the Sylvan landscape, and brimming with dynamic activities, the 12,000 sq.ft. clubhouse offers you an unparalleled lifestyle. Conceived by Studio HBA, world renowned architects, it is designed for the fastidous few.

Residences in vogue (and surely in some interior magazines)

Off-limits to all, except the residents of Lodha Estilo, 2 acres of open space, boasting a rich biodiversity and a delightful medley of experiences, provides the perfect grounds for residents to open up to each other, and make friends of the same wavelength.
